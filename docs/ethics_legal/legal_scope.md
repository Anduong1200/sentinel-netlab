# Legal Scope & Compliance

**WARNING**: Capabilities provided by Sentinel NetLab (Monitor Mode, Frame Injection) are powerful tools that can violate local and international laws if misused.

## 1. Authorized Use Only
- **Ownership**: You must own the equipment and the network you are testing.
- **Consent**: You must have explicit written consent from the network owner if you do not own it.
- **Scope**: Testing is strictly limited to the SSID/BSSID specified in the authorization scope.

## 2. Prohibited Actions
- **Interference**: Jamming or deauthenticating clients on public networks (Coffee shops, Airports) is **illegal** (e.g., US 47 U.S.C. ยง 333).
- **Eavesdropping**: Capturing unencrypted data payloads from third parties is a violation of wiretap laws (e.g., US 18 U.S.C. ยง 2511).
